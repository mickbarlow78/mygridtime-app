App name: MyGridTime



What it does:

Helps users plan, track and optimise how they use their time.



Target user:

Individuals and small business owners who want better time visibility and control.



Core features (V1 only):

\- Time tracking dashboard

\- Simple activity logging

\- Basic reporting

\- User login



Pages:

\- Home

\- Dashboard

\- Login

\- Reports

\- AI features

\- Integrations

\- Mobile app
